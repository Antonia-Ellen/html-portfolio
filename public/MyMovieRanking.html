The Best Movies According to Ellen

My top 3 films of all-time

Pride and prejudice(2005)

A witty and headstrong Elizabeth Bennet clashes with the proud and reserved Mr. Darcy in rural England, overcoming societal pressures and misunderstandings to find unexpected love.

Legally blonde(2001)

A determined and fashionable sorority queen, underestimated by everyone, enrolls in Harvard Law School to win back her ex-boyfriend, only to discover her own true potential and passion for the law.

The edge of seventeen(2016)

A self-absorbed and socially awkward high school junior feels her world is ending when her popular older brother starts dating her best friend, forcing her to navigate life and loneliness on her own.
